About Me

Hi y’all! My name is Rob Chant and I am a student in my first year of Algonquin College’s Interactive Media Design program. I’ve studied at the College before as well – in 2012 I received a certificate for completing the General Arts and Science – Introduction to Fine Arts program. From there, I went on to the University of Ottawa, where I received a Bachelor of Fine Arts specializing in painting and drawing. I definitely like to get creative!

Outside of school I have worked in retail management for most of my adult life. I’m currently a Floor Leader for Lush Cosmetics, and love working for the brand. I live with my partner and dog in Vanier, and in my spare time I love to practice yoga. I’m also super into astrology: I'm a Leo sun, Virgo moon and Capricorn rising. Otherwise, I enjoy tarot reading, and have spent a lot of time carving my own linocut prints lately.

... and in the infamous words of a very wise woman:
